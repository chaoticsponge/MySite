Perhentian

I recently spent several unforgettable days on the Perhentian Islands in Malaysia, and it turned out to be one of the best travel experiences I’ve ever had. Between learning diving and exploring the island with my friends, this tropical getaway offered everything I could ask for—crystal-clear waters, colorful marine life, and an easygoing island vibe that makes you never want to leave.

Learning to Dive with Anti-Gravity Divers

The highlight of my trip was completing my Open Water Course with Anti-Gravity Divers. The instructors were super friendly and patient. Since my brother and I were picking it up quick, they took us to a LOT of dive sites instead of teching us on the shore.

The underwater view was amazing, each dive introduced me to a new world: vibrant coral gardens, schools of fish swirling like silver clouds, and even the occasional turtle gliding by as if in slow motion. The visibility was incredible, often over 20 meters, with warm, calm water that made every dive effortless. It's like I was in Nemo!

Staying at Sha-Ri La Resort

My stay at Sha-Ri La Resort was perfect for a balance of comfort and island simplicity. The resort sits right by the beach with easy access to it's own private beach, the dive shop, restaurants, and snorkelling spots.

Snorkelling and Beach Days

When I wasn’t diving, I spent time snorkelling just off the beach. The marine life was rich and diverse—parrotfish, clownfish, rays, and even baby reef sharks. The water was so clear that visibility was nearly perfect from the surface. The beaches here are pristine, and the calm, shallow lagoons make it ideal for swimming.

Bars and Nightlife

Nights on the Perhentian was mellow and full of charm. After sunset, the beaches come alive with music, laughter, and amazing fire shows. Most of the action happens around Long Beach, where a handful of laid-back bars set up beanbags in the sand and serve ice-cold drinks under the stars. It’s the kind of nightlife that fits the island—casual, social, and scenic. A few places stay open late, with reggae beats or acoustic sets. Great place to think about slowing down, meeting new people, and enjoying the moment.

Final Thoughts

If you’re dreaming of a laid-back tropical escape with clear blue water, friendly people, and world-class diving, Perhentian Malaysia should be at the top of your list.
