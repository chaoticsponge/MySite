My Journey

The main reason I really got into cybersecurity was because of Covid! All the news popping up about cyberattacks everywhere made me realize how vulnerable our digital lives are. It made me a lot more privacy concious as a person in a digital sense. Channels like Louis Rossman, Techlore and many others got me started in degoogling my life, learning to self-host my services, and taking control of my online presence.

It was also around the same time I met Mena, a minecraft server owner who made custom servers and needed a hand. He really inspired me to dive deeper into the technical aspects of server management and programming. We continued doing custom servers and websites together as a side-hustle which helped me further my IT skills.

It's more the combination of being more privacy concious and learning to program which got my hands dirty with software. It made me want to pursue a career in cybersecurity. At the time I was doing my degree in Genomics at Monash University. Jumping from Genomics to IT is quite the reach but one I'm glad I took.
